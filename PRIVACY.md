## Overview
Cycle Companion helps you track cycle information and (optionally) share updates with your partner. We collect only the data needed to provide the app’s core features. We do not sell your data.

## Data We Collect
Depending on how you use the app, we may collect:
- **Account data:** email address, display name, and authentication identifiers.
- **App data you provide:** cycle events, settings, partner nickname, messages, and preferences.
- **Device/technical data:** device identifiers for push notifications, app version, and basic diagnostic data.

## How We Use Your Data
We use your data to:
- Create and maintain your account.
- Sync your settings and cycle data across your devices (if you enable cloud sync).
- Deliver notifications you opt in to.
- Provide support and troubleshoot issues.

## Data Sharing
- **With your partner:** only when you choose to connect and share.
- **Service providers:** we use Firebase (Google) for authentication, database, storage, and notifications. These providers process data on our behalf.
- **We do not sell your data.**

## Data Storage
Your data is stored in Firebase (Google Cloud).  
If you enable cloud sync, your widget settings and related images are stored in cloud storage.

## Data Retention
We keep your data as long as you have an account. You can request deletion at any time.

## Your Choices
You can:
- Edit or delete data in the app.
- Disable cloud sync for widget settings.
- Disconnect your partner.
- Request account deletion by contacting us.

## Children’s Privacy
Cycle Companion is not intended for children under 13 (or the age defined by your local law). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## International Transfers
Your data may be processed in countries where our service providers operate.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy occasionally. We will update the “Effective date” above.
